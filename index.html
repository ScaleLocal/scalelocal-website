COMMON QUESTIONS

Everything you want to know
before you reach out.

If something isn't answered here, ask us directly — we'll give you a straight answer.

How long before I start seeing results?
+
Most clients see their first inbound leads within the first two to four weeks of launch. The Foundation System guarantee is 3 leads per month by day 60 — no ad spend required. Momentum and Authority clients typically see results faster because paid channels activate alongside organic. The 60 and 90-day ramp periods are built into your contract as the measurement window, but in practice most accounts move faster than that.
What if you don't hit the guaranteed numbers?
+
We keep working for free until we do. That's not a talking point — it's written into your service agreement. If we miss your monthly lead minimum within the ramp period, your billing pauses and we continue full service at no charge until we hit the target. We've never had to invoke it, but the fact that it's in your contract tells you everything about our confidence in the system.
Do I need to have an ad budget?
+
Not for Foundation. The Foundation System generates leads entirely through organic channels — your website, Google Business Profile, local SEO, and reviews. No ad spend required. Momentum and Authority include managed paid campaigns, and those guarantees require a minimum ad budget ($500/mo and $1,000/mo respectively) because paid performance is directly tied to spend. Ad dollars are billed directly to your account — we never mark them up.
What's the commitment? Can I cancel?
+
All plans are 6-month agreements. After 6 months you can cancel with 30 days notice. Month-to-month is available with a one-time $497 setup fee. The 6-month commitment exists because local SEO and map pack ranking take time to compound — we need the runway to deliver on the guarantee. We don't lock you in beyond that.
How is ScaleLocal different from other local marketing agencies?
+
Three things most agencies won't do: publish their pricing, put a written lead guarantee in the contract, and give you a live dashboard instead of a monthly PDF. We also don't have account coordinators — you work with Matt or someone he's personally trained. We're intentionally small because the quality of work drops the moment an agency takes on more clients than it can actually serve.
What types of businesses do you work with?
+
Any local service business where a new customer has real dollar value — HVAC, plumbing, electrical, dental, medical, roofing, contracting, auto repair, legal, financial services, salons, fitness. We work across Massachusetts and New Hampshire. If you're a local business that depends on people finding you online, ScaleLocal was built for you.
What does the onboarding process look like?
+
Day 1 you get your free Digital Presence Snapshot. Our team reviews it personally and your personal account manager delivers a clear plan — showing exactly what we'd do for your business and why. Once you're signed, your website is live within 48 hours. Every tool in your system — tracking, dashboard, booking, review funnel — is configured and tested before we consider you launched. Your personal account manager is on your account from day one, not week four.
What happens after the 6-month agreement ends?
+
You keep everything — your website, your rankings, your reviews, your dashboard access. If you continue with ScaleLocal, we move to a month-to-month agreement with 30 days notice to cancel. Most clients stay because the system keeps compounding. The leads don't stop when the contract ends — and neither do we, as long as you want us.

The ScaleLocal Performance Guarantee

The following language is included verbatim in every ScaleLocal Service Agreement:

Section 4 — Performance Guarantee

ScaleLocal guarantees Client will receive no fewer than [TIER MINIMUM] qualified leads per month by the end of the Guarantee Ramp Period, as defined below. A "qualified lead" is defined as a tracked inbound call, form submission, or booking request originating from ScaleLocal-managed channels and recorded in the Client's ScaleLocal Dashboard.

Guarantee Ramp Period: Foundation System — 60 days from official launch date. Momentum and Authority Systems — 90 days from official launch date.

Remedy: If ScaleLocal fails to deliver the guaranteed minimum by the end of the Ramp Period, ScaleLocal will continue providing all services at no charge to Client until the guaranteed minimum is achieved for one full calendar month. No refunds are issued in lieu of continued service.

Conditions: The Performance Guarantee is contingent on Client maintaining the minimum recommended ad spend for their tier (if applicable), providing timely access to required accounts and assets, and remaining current on all payments. The guarantee is void if Client pauses, reduces, or cancels ad spend below the stated minimum.

This language appears in every service agreement before signing. You will receive a full copy of your agreement via DocuSign before any payment is processed.
